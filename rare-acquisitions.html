Rare Acquisitions

The piece you want
exists. We find it.

The most coveted objects in the world carry waiting lists of three to five years — or they simply do not appear for sale through standard channels at all. A crocodile Birkin in a specific colour. A Royal Oak perpetual calendar in titanium. A first-edition Patek Philippe reference that surfaces in private hands perhaps once a decade. These pieces are not found. They are accessed through people who know when they become available and who to call in the first five minutes.

ILOVELUX maintains active relationships with senior boutique contacts, private collectors and specialist traders across every category of ultra-rare objects. When a piece of significance changes hands, we know before it is offered to anyone outside a very small circle. We act swiftly, quietly and with the certainty that comes from trust built over years. Most acquisitions are completed within days of engagement.
