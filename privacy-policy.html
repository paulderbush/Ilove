Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Our commitment to your privacy

ILOVELUX is built on discretion. We do not collect, store, sell or share your personal data with any third parties. This is not a legal formality — it is the foundation of how we operate.

We understand that those who use our services value their privacy above all else. Every interaction with ILOVELUX is treated with absolute confidentiality.

What we do not do

  • We do not store your personal information beyond what is necessary to fulfil your request
  • We do not sell, rent or share your data with third parties
  • We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes
  • We do not build profiles or databases of our clients
  • We do not use your information for any purpose other than responding to your enquiry

Information you provide to us

When you contact us via our website, WhatsApp or email, you may voluntarily provide your name, contact details and a description of the service you are interested in. This information is used solely to respond to your request and coordinate the service.

Once a service is completed, any personal information provided is deleted from our systems unless you have explicitly requested an ongoing relationship or further services.

Third-party service providers

In the course of delivering a service, we may need to engage third-party suppliers (hotels, aviation providers, event organisers, etc.). In such cases, only the minimum necessary information is shared, strictly for the purpose of fulfilling your request. We work exclusively with partners who adhere to strict confidentiality standards.

Communication security

We recommend using encrypted communication channels (WhatsApp, Signal, or encrypted email) for sensitive requests. All communications received by ILOVELUX are treated as strictly confidential and are not disclosed to any party without your explicit consent.

Your rights

You have the right to request confirmation of what information (if any) we hold about you, and to request its deletion at any time. Simply contact us at hello@ilovelux.com and we will respond within 48 hours.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after any change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
